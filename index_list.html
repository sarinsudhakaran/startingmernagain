Breakfast

Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Pour the mixture into a heated pan.
  4. Add cheese and vegetables if desired.
  5. Cook until the eggs are set.
